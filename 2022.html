The Journey So Far
June
In June, we organized a career talk and had career discussions with the children living with disabilities at Chesire home, Sango, Ibadan. We invited professionals including Nigeria’s first blind DJ to share opinions on the various career opportunities available regardless of the limitations.




September
For the second edition of the annual “back to school” outreach, in September 2022, we gave school materials to over 40 students in a community school in Ogeye, Ibadan.




December
For our anniversary in 2022, we visited the Living Word Missions home, Okebola, Ibadan for a clothe drive with over 20 children.
