The Journey So Far
In December 2019 when we began our work, we started with a 2-day project. The first day was a workshop with residents of Chesire home, Sango, Ibadan where we taught the craft of paper bags.

The second day was a visit to a juvenile home in Sango, Ibadan where we donated basic foodstuff and clothing.




We also started the One meal challenge, which was a way of generating funds. This challenge allowed people to send to us whatever amount they spent on one meal. After careful consideration, we decided to make it a consistent challenge every month
