Our History
We began our work in December 2019 with a 2-day project.
The first day was a workshop with the residents of Cheshire Home where we taught crafting of paper bags.
The second day was a visit to a juvenile home in Sango, Ibadan where we donated basic foodstuff and clothing. Since then we've been taking up more projects all for the empowerment and education of children and young adults.
