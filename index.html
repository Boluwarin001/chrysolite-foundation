REACHING FOR THE STARS: TILL THE FIFTH & BEYOND (A volunteer’s POV)

Make no mistake. This is not just a story of one woman. It is a story of a dream fuelled by the passion of this one woman and supported by hundreds of other people.
Thus, the Chrysolite Foundation came into being in December, 2019. A team composed of mostly young university students, burdened with the task of empowering children and young adults in low-income communities with needed skills for intellectual growth and development.
Each outreach meant that many stars had to be reached and polished. Every strategy, every teardrop, and every single naira spent resulted in a great profit for our stars; our profit was seeing the resulting smiles, satisfaction, growth and self-confidence.


Our History

We began our work in December 2019 and have since then been committed to providing young people in underprivileged communities with access to empowerment and education opportunities.
